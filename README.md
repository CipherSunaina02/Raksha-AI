# Raksha AI — Citizen Fraud Shield

AI-powered platform that detects digital arrest scams in real time and maps fraud 
networks before money is lost.

## Problem Statement
AI for Digital Public Safety — detecting and disrupting digital fraud 
networks, focusing on "digital arrest" scam patterns and fraud ring mapping.

## About
Raksha AI analyzes call patterns and transaction signals to detect "digital arrest" 
scams as they happen — flagging impersonation, isolation tactics, and urgency cues 
before a victim transfers money. It maps connected fraud accounts into networks and 
generates audit-ready evidence packages for law enforcement.
